About Us

KES Shroff College, established in 1990, is a leading institution in Maharashtra, offering quality education in arts, science, and commerce. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a global society.

History of Our College

Is a pioneer Educational Trust registered under society’s registration Act 1860, and public Trust Act 1950 (Mumbai). Founded in the year 1936. Organization well known for its various educational services for more than 7 decades. Spreading education to more than 9000 students. The change in socio-economic condition and on public demand it started the technical section in 1962. In 1970 it started the Night High School to provide the students opportunity to study along with service. Sangeet Mahavidyalaya started in the year 1984 to develop aesthetic aspirations of the students and youth to impart training in vocal and instrumental music. As an addition to its activities, Kandivli Education Society’s Junior college in June 1976 and upgraded it into a Degree college of Arts & Commerce in 1989. S.V.P. Jr. College Science renamed as T.P. Bhatia Jr. College of Science in 2005. Started ‘A’ Level KES’ CAMBRIDGE INTERNATIONAL JUNIOR COLLEGE (Std XI & XII) Arts, Science and Commerce.

Vision and Mission

Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized institution of excellence in higher education, fostering innovation, research, and lifelong learning. We aim to empower students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values to become responsible global citizens and leaders in their respective fields. Empowering the youth to build their destiny by moulding their character and respecting their right to learn and power to earn.

Mission

To provide high-quality education through innovative teaching methodologies and a dynamic curriculum. To create a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving. To promote interdisciplinary research and collaborations for societal development. To instill ethical values, leadership qualities, and a sense of social responsibility in students. To bridge the gap between academia and industry by enhancing employability skills. To foster diversity, inclusivity, and equal opportunities for all students. To engage with the community through outreach programs and social initiatives. To embrace technological advancements and digital learning for enhanced educational experiences. To uphold sustainability and environmental consciousness in all institutional activities. To continuously evolve as a center of academic excellence, inspiring lifelong learning and innovation. To provide a transformative education that fosters intellectual growth, ethical leadership, and social responsibility.
